Setup Instructions

Get Added to Team_Members Sheet

Contact your Technical Lead to add you with your Discord ID, Name, and Role.

Enable Discord Direct Messages

Go to Discord Settings → Privacy & Safety → Enable "Allow direct messages from server members"

Enable Developer Mode (Optional)

Settings → Advanced → Enable Developer Mode for enhanced features

Available Commands

/task [desc]
Log tasks (use ; to separate multiple tasks)
/update_task [desc]
Update an existing task description
/completed [task]

/pending [task]
Mark a task as pending or in-progress

Daily Workflow

Morning Routine

Receive a DM at 10:00 UTC. Respond with:

  • /task to log your daily tasks
  • /challenge if facing any issues
  • /blocker for critical roadblocks

Evening Routine

Receive status request at 16:00 UTC. Update with:

  • /completed for finished tasks
  • /pending for ongoing work
  • /update_task to modify descriptions

Blocker Escalation Process

Report the Blocker

Send: /blocker I need an API key for the production environment

Technical Lead Notified

Your Technical Lead receives an immediate notification with your blocker details

Lead Responds

The Lead replies directly to the bot via DM with a solution or next steps

You Get the Response

Receive the Lead's response in your DM, resolving your blocker quickly

Troubleshooting

Profile Not Found

If you receive a "profile not found" message, your account hasn't been added to the system yet.

Solution: Contact your Technical Lead to add you to Team_Members sheet.

Not Receiving DMs

If the bot isn't sending you direct messages, your Discord privacy settings may be blocking it.

Solution: Enable "Allow direct messages from server members" in Discord Settings → Privacy & Safety.
